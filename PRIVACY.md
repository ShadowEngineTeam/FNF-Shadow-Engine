# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 9, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Shadow Engine.

## Data Collection

**Shadow Engine does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The application operates entirely offline and does not:

- Collect analytics or telemetry
- Send crash reports
- Track user behavior
- Access personal information
- Communicate with external servers
- Request internet permissions

## Local Storage

The only data stored is saved locally on your device for game functionality:

- **Game settings** (volume, display preferences, controls, etc.)
- **High scores and progress** (song scores, week completion)

This data never leaves your device. You can delete it at any time through the game's settings or by removing the app data in your device settings.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised.
